Implementation Roadmap
3-phase execution plan — Months 1 through Year 3
3 Phases
Phase 1 · Month 1
PairD Global Audit
CTO commissions full usage audit across all European offices. Baseline KPI established. Owner: CTO.
KPI: Usage baseline set
Phase 1 · Month 2–3
AI Onboarding Rollout — All Regions
Structured onboarding programme deployed globally. Regional MDs own 90% completion target within 90 days.
KPI: 90% completion rate
Phase 2 · Month 5–7
Deloitte Knowledge Hub Launch
AI-powered universal search + centralised repository replacing siloed office systems. AI Institute leads delivery.
KPI: 35% search time reduction
Phase 2 · Month 10–12
Share & Connect Platform
Peer knowledge-sharing network. Replaces email threads with structured knowledge exchanges. KM Lead owns rollout.
KPI: 95% satisfaction score
Phase 3 · Year 1
Deloitte LearnAI Unit Launch
Dedicated AI upskilling unit modelled on Accenture LearnVantage. CLO leads. All consultants on personalised pathways.
KPI: 100% certified by Year 2
Phase 3 · Year 2–3
AI Coaching Agents Deployed
Personalised AI coaches per consultant role. Promotion criteria formally linked to AI fluency. CHRO owns programme.
KPI: 5%+ EBIT improvement
